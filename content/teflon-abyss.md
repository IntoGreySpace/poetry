Hello, I'm here\
allow me to introduce myself\
I'm Fear.

What, your hair is a mess?\
Why do you need to piss?\
How is this?\
Have you slipped into the teflon abyss?

You blindly navigate\
plotting a course beyond your control\
you need a mate\
you need a goal.

Why is my brain amiss?\
Have I slipped into the teflon abyss?

You grab at every hold\
doing what you've been told\
just wait 'till you get old.

...until we meet again\
you can think clearly\
but then...\
I'll have you dearly.
