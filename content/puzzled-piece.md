Name = "A Puzzled Piece"
Date = "1991-06-10"
+++

All this, it matters naught\
I look around, at all I've got

This stuff, arranged so neat\
and still, it remains incomplete

because my life, a puzzled piece\
fits together so seamly sweet.

And yet I know\
when its done\
the puzzle will be complete.

But that day will never come\
for me\
because I've finally come to see\
that I'm\
the missing piece.
